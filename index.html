The Jargon for Coffee Beans.

Rosat Levels

In general, there are 3 roast levels that are mostly seen on the market: light, medium, and dark roast. How long the beans are being roasted will drastically affect the taste of the cup it brews.

Light Roast

Light roast intends to preserve a coffee bean's natural aromas and flavors, because they have been roasted for a shorter period of time. Lightly roasted coffee has high acidity levels. Because the flavor of lightly roasted beans is underdeveloped compared to darker roasts, the flavor may occur as toasted, and grain-like.

    Characteristics:
  • High acidity
  • Toasty, grain-like taste
  • Fruity smell
